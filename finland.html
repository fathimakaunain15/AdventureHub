Finland — Northern Lights & Lapland Magic

Aurora skies, igloo hotels, snow forests, reindeer safaris & the dreamland of winter.

Quick Overview

Finland is one of the world’s best destinations for Northern Lights, especially in Lapland. With snowy forests, glass igloos, Santa Village, frozen lakes and luxury winter stays, it’s a fairytale winter trip.

Top Places to Visit

  • Lapland — Northern Lights & Igloo resorts
  • Rovaniemi — Santa Claus Village
  • Helsinki — Cathedral, markets & design district
  • Sauna Lakes — Finnish spa + ice dipping
  • Levi — Skiing & snow adventures
  • Frozen Waterfalls & Snow Forests
