Why Goa for Honeymoon?

Goa is perfect for couples wanting romance + fun — luxury beach resorts, sunset points, island tours, seafood cafes, nightlife and private villas make it unforgettable.

Romantic Things to Do

  • Candlelight dinner by the beach
  • Private boat cruise
  • Couple spa & massage
  • Snorkeling & scuba diving
  • Visit Butterfly Beach

Best Areas for Couples

  • South Goa — peaceful, luxury resorts
  • Benaulim & Colva — romantic sunsets
  • Palolem — quiet & beautiful
  • Calangute / Baga — nightlife lovers

3-Day Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1 — Beach Time & Candlelight Dinner

Relax at a luxury beach resort. Enjoy couple spa. End the day with romantic candlelight dinner.

Day 2 — Island Trip & Water Sports

Take a private island tour (Grande Island). Enjoy snorkeling, dolphin watching & sunset boating.

Day 3 — Shopping & Old Goa

Visit Old Goa churches, spice plantations and enjoy beach photography.
