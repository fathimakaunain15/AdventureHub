Coorg — The Coffee Hills of India

Foggy mornings, green forests, coffee plantations, waterfalls & peaceful hideaways.

Best time: Oct–Apr Ideal trip: 2–4 days Perfect for: Couples & Nature Lovers

Quick Overview

Coorg (Kodagu) is one of Karnataka’s most beautiful hill stations, famous for its coffee plantations, misty hills, forests, rivers & spice estates. Ideal for couples, families, and peaceful getaways.

Top Places to Visit

  • Abbey Falls
  • Mandapatti Peak View
  • Coffee Plantation Tours
  • Raja’s Seat Sunset Point
  • Talacauvery Temple
  • Nisargadhama
  • Dubare Elephant Camp

3-Day Coorg Itinerary

Day 1 — Abbey Falls & Raja’s Seat

Visit Abbey Falls, explore local shops & enjoy sunset at Raja’s Seat.

Day 2 — Coffee Plantations

Take a coffee tour, Mandalpatti Jeep Safari & nature walks.

Day 3 — Dubare & Cauvery

Visit Dubare Elephant Camp, Nisargadhama & Talacauvery.
