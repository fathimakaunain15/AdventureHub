Maldives — Water Villas & Crystal Lagoons

Private islands, overwater villas, coral reefs, snorkeling and world-class resorts.

Island Nation Best Time: Nov – Apr Snorkeling • Diving • Luxury

Quick Overview

The Maldives is a tropical paradise of coral atolls and turquoise lagoons — ideal for honeymooners, divers and luxury travellers. Expect crystal-clear water, vibrant marine life, and private island resorts.

3-Day Luxury Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival, Resort Transfer & Relax

Seaplane or speedboat transfer to your private island resort. Sunset walk and spa recovery.

Day 2 — Snorkeling & Water Sports

Guided snorkeling at the house reef, optional scuba dive, paddleboarding and sunset cruise.

Day 3 — Island Hopping & Romantic Dinner

Visit nearby sandbank, picnic on a deserted isle and private beach dinner under stars.

Diving & Marine Life

The Maldives is home to colorful coral gardens, manta rays, whale sharks (seasonal) and abundant reef fish. Choose a PADI-certified dive centre, check visibility and protected areas, and follow reef-safe sunscreen practices.

How to Reach

  • Fly to Malé (Velana International Airport)
  • Transfer by seaplane or speedboat to island resorts
  • Season: November–April for best weather and calm seas

Packing & Tips

  • Light cotton clothing, swimwear & reef-safe sunscreen.
  • Snorkel mask (optional), waterproof bag for phones.
  • Respect local customs on inhabited islands (cover shoulders/legs).
  • Book transfers early; seaplane schedules depend on tide/daylight.
