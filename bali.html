Bali — Temples, Rice Terraces & Beach Vibes

Ubud rice terraces, cliff temples, surf beaches, waterfalls and sunset vibes.

Indonesia Best Time: Apr – Sep Temples • Surf • Nature

Quick Overview

Bali blends lush rice terraces, dramatic cliffs, spiritual temples and golden beaches. Perfect for nature lovers, surfers, photographers and honeymooners. Ubud is the cultural heart; Uluwatu, Seminyak and Canggu serve beaches and nightlife.

3-Day Suggested Itinerary

Day 1 — Ubud & Rice Terraces

Morning: Tegalalang rice terraces & swing. Afternoon: Ubud Monkey Forest, local art markets. Evening: Balinese dance performance.

Day 2 — Temples & Waterfalls

Visit Tirta Empul (holy spring), Tegenungan Waterfall, then explore local cafes. Sunset at Tanah Lot or Uluwatu with Kecak dance.

Day 3 — Beaches & Sunset

Head to southern beaches: Padang Padang, Bingin or Seminyak for surf and sunsets. Evening: beach clubs in Seminyak/Canggu.

Things To Do

  • Rice terraces & sunrise photography
  • Temple visits (respect dress code — sarong)
  • Surfing or lessons at Kuta / Canggu
  • Balinese spa & yoga in Ubud
  • Visit local markets for handicrafts & coffee (kopi luwak)

Top Villas & Resorts

Cliff-edge Villas (Uluwatu)

Sunset balconies & private pools.

Ubud Jungle Retreat

Rice-terrace views, yoga & spa.

How to Reach

  • Fly to Ngurah Rai International Airport (Denpasar)
  • Hire a car/taxi or local driver for island travel (recommended)
  • Best months: April–September (drier season)

Map

Packing & Local Tips

  • Light cotton clothing, reef-safe sunscreen & insect repellent.
  • Respect temple rules — cover shoulders & knees; carry a sarong.
  • Bargain gently in markets; carry small cash.
  • Rent a scooter only if experienced — roads can be busy.
