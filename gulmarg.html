Gulmarg — Snow, Gondola & Ski Paradise

World-class skiing, snow meadows, Gulmarg Gondola & breathtaking winter landscapes.

Quick Overview

Gulmarg is India’s top skiing destination with powder snow, pine forests, vast meadows & the famous Gulmarg Gondola. A perfect winter paradise for couples, adventure travellers & snow lovers.

Top Attractions

  • Gulmarg Gondola — Asia’s highest cable car
  • Apharwat Peak — best snow & skiing
  • Strawberry Valley
  • Kongdori Meadows
  • Snow biking & ATV rides
  • St. Mary’s Church
