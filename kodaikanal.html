Kodaikanal — Misty Mountains

Rolling hills, waterfalls, clouds touching the earth — Kodaikanal is Tamil Nadu’s magical mist heaven.

Best time: Oct–Jun Ideal trip: 2–4 days Altitude: 2,133 m

Quick Overview

Known as “The Princess of Hill Stations,” Kodaikanal is famous for its foggy mornings, lush valleys, pine forests, chocolate shops, lakes and spectacular viewpoints. A dreamy escape for couples, families and nature lovers.

Top Places to Visit

  • Kodaikanal Lake
  • Pine Forest
  • Coaker’s Walk
  • Pillar Rocks
  • Guna Caves (Devil’s Kitchen)
  • Silver Cascade Waterfall
  • Bryant Park

3-Day Kodaikanal Itinerary

Day 1 — Lake & Coaker’s Walk

Arrive, stroll at Coaker’s Walk, explore Bryant Park & enjoy a boat ride.

Day 2 — Pine Forest & Guna Caves

Visit Pine Forest, Guna Caves, and viewpoints like Pillar Rocks.

Day 3 — Silver Cascade & Shopping

Relax near waterfalls, explore cafes, try homemade chocolates, depart.
