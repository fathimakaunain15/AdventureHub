Why Seychelles is a Dream Honeymoon?

Seychelles is one of the most exclusive honeymoon destinations in the world. Its untouched nature, white-powder beaches, clear blue waters and luxury resorts make it perfect for couples looking for: privacy, beauty and relaxation.

Romantic Highlights

  • Pink-sand beach walks at sunrise
  • Private ocean-front dinners
  • Couple spa treatments overlooking the sea
  • Catamaran sailing with dolphins
  • Snorkeling & underwater adventures

Most Beautiful Islands

  • Mahé – main island
  • Praslin – famous beaches
  • La Digue – iconic granite rocks
  • Silhouette Island – ultra luxury

3-Day Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Secret Beaches

Relax at Anse Intendance or Beau Vallon Beach. Enjoy a private dinner arranged by your resort.

Day 2 — La Digue & Anse Source d’Argent

Visit the most photographed beach in the world. Explore La Digue on bicycles, capture honeymoon pictures.

Day 3 — Praslin & Snorkeling

Visit Vallée de Mai forest and take a catamaran cruise for snorkeling with fishes and turtles.

How to Reach

  • Take ferries to Praslin & La Digue.
