Why Andaman for Honeymoon?

Andaman is one of India’s most romantic beach destinations — crystal-clear water, peaceful beaches, luxurious private stays, and world-class scuba diving.

Romantic Activities

  • Couple photoshoot at Radhanagar Beach
  • Private candlelight dinner
  • Scuba diving & snorkeling
  • Sunset cruise
  • Visit Ross & Neil islands

Best Islands

  • Havelock — best for couples
  • Neil Island — quiet & beautiful
  • Port Blair — city & museums
  • Ross Island — historical ruins

3-Day Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1 — Havelock Island & Beach Time

Reach Havelock Island, check into a sea-facing resort, relax at Radhanagar Beach, candlelight dinner.

Day 2 — Elephant Beach & Water Sports

Enjoy snorkeling, scuba diving & glass-bottom boat ride. Visit Neil Island for sunset.

Day 3 — Port Blair & Ross Island

Visit Ross Island ruins, light & sound show at Cellular Jail, romantic dinner by the sea.
