Andaman Islands — Turquoise Paradise

Crystal clear water, white sand beaches, coral reefs and romantic sunsets — Andaman is India’s ultimate island escape.

Best time: Oct–May Ideal trip: 5–7 days Perfect for: Honeymoon & Family

Quick Overview

The Andaman Islands are a group of tropical islands in the Bay of Bengal, known for their clear blue water, colourful marine life, beautiful beaches and laid-back island life. The main hubs are Port Blair, Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) and Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep).

Top Places to Visit

  • Radhanagar Beach (Havelock) – one of Asia’s most beautiful beaches
  • Elephant Beach – water sports & snorkelling
  • Kalapathar Beach – sunrise views & photo spots
  • Neil Island – Laxmanpur, Bharatpur & Natural Bridge
  • Cellular Jail (Port Blair) – light & sound show
  • Ross Island – ruins & scenic walks

5-Day Andaman Itinerary

Day 1 — Port Blair

Arrive in Port Blair, visit Cellular Jail & enjoy the light-and-sound show.

Day 2 — Havelock & Radhanagar

Ferry to Havelock, check-in, relax at Radhanagar Beach till sunset.

Day 3 — Elephant Beach & Water Sports

Go to Elephant Beach for snorkelling, glass-bottom boat & jet ski.

Day 4 — Neil Island

Visit Bharatpur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach & Natural Bridge; stay overnight or return.

Day 5 — Ross Island & Departure

Back to Port Blair, explore Ross Island (Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Island), depart.

How to Reach Andaman

  • By Air: Direct flights to Port Blair from major Indian cities (Chennai, Kolkata, Bangalore, Delhi via connection).
  • By Sea: Ship services from Chennai, Visakhapatnam & Kolkata (takes several days).
  • Local Travel: Inter-island ferries between Port Blair, Havelock & Neil.
