Why Visit Nice?

Nice is the heart of the French Riviera — filled with pastel streets, blue sea views, French cafés, cliffside villages and luxurious Mediterranean vibes.

Top Attractions

  • Promenade des Anglais
  • Old Town (Vieux Nice)
  • Castle Hill Viewpoint
  • Blue Beach & Villefranche Bay
  • Éze Village (15 min drive)

Nearby Riviera Icons

  • Monaco — Casinos & Formula 1
  • Cannes — Film Festival city
  • Saint-Tropez — Luxury port

3-Day French Riviera Adventure

Day 1 — Nice City + Castle Hill

Stroll Promenade des Anglais, explore Old Town and finish with sunset at Castle Hill.

Day 2 — Monaco & Èze

Visit hilltop Èze Village, then explore Monaco’s casinos, marina & royal palace.

Day 3 — Cannes & Beaches

Relax at French Riviera beaches and visit Cannes’ luxurious film festival boulevard.
