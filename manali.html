Manali – Ultimate Travel Guide

Overview

Manali is one of India’s most beautiful mountain destinations — filled with snow-peaks, pine forests, riverside cafés, and adventure activities. It's ideal for couples, families, trekkers, and nature lovers.

Photo Gallery

Best Time to Visit

  • March – June: Pleasant weather, adventure sports.
  • Sept – Nov: Golden forests, clear skies.
  • Dec – Feb: SNOWFALL season — the best time for snow lovers.

Top Places to Visit

  • Solang Valley – snow & adventure capital
  • Old Manali – riverside cafés, nightlife, boho vibe
  • Naggar Castle – scenic heritage fort
  • Hadimba Temple – iconic forest temple
  • Atal Tunnel & Sissu – stunning winter landscapes
  • Vashisht – hot springs & viewpoints

5-Day Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Old Manali + riverside cafés
  2. Day 2: Solang Valley (snow activities)
  3. Day 3: Atal Tunnel + Sissu waterfalls
  4. Day 4: Naggar Castle + Jana Waterfall
  5. Day 5: Vashisht + local shopping + cafés

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm clothes always — nights are cold.
  • Snow season roads may delay — keep buffer time.
  • Book hotels early in December–January.
  • Avoid riverbank areas at night.
