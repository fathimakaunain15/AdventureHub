Quick Overview

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan town with year-round adventure. From snowy slopes at Rohtang to paragliding at Solang, Manali suits families, solo travellers and thrill-seekers alike.

Top Attractions

  • Solang Valley — paragliding, skiing, zorbing
  • Rohtang Pass — snow panorama (permit required)
  • Hadimba Temple — peaceful forest temple
  • Jogini Waterfall — short scenic trek
  • Old Manali & Mall Road — cafes & nightlife

Quick Tips

  • Carry layers — weather changes fast.
  • Rohtang permit: apply online early (if visiting).
  • Cash is handy in small shops; ATMs available in town.
  • Respect local culture & mountains — avoid littering.

3-Day Best Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival, Mall Road & Hadimba

Arrive in Manali (Bhuntar airport / bus). Check-in, relax. Evening: stroll Mall Road, try local cafe food.

Day 2 — Solang Valley & Adventure

Full day at Solang Valley: paragliding, ropeway, zorbing. Evening: Old Manali cafes & bonfire.

Day 3 — Rohtang Pass / Jogini Waterfall

Visit Rohtang Pass (if open) for snow & photos. If closed, visit Jogini Waterfall & local markets.

Things To Do — Activities & Adventure

Adventure

  • Skiing & snowboarding (seasonal)
  • Paragliding at Solang Valley
  • River rafting on Beas (nearby seasonally)
  • Short treks & mountain photography

Relax & Culture

  • Visit Hadimba Temple & Van Vihar
  • Explore Mall Road & old bazaars
  • Stay at a homestay to experience local food

Quick Facts

Altitude

≈ 2,050 m (6,726 ft)

Best Season

Mar–Jun (pleasant), Dec–Feb (snow)

Top Hotels

Snow Valley Resorts

Comfortable stay, good views. From budget to mid-range.

Zostel Manali

Budget-friendly, great for solo travellers and groups.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Bhuntar (Kullu) Airport ~50 km
  • By Train: Joginder Nagar (toy train nearby) / Pathankot or Chandigarh + road
  • By Road: Regular buses/volvos from Delhi (12–14 hrs)

Local Contacts

Tour operators, ski rentals and guide services are available in Mall Road & Solang Valley (book locally).

Safety & Packing

  • Carry warm layers, gloves & thermal wear.
  • Sunscreen & sunglasses (snow glare).
  • Check vehicle permits for Rohtang before planning.
  • Keep chargers & power bank; mobile signals can be weak.
  • Respect local rules; avoid venturing into closed areas.
