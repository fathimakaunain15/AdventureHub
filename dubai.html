Dubai — Luxury Desert City

Skyscrapers, beaches, deserts, shopping, luxury hotels & futuristic adventures.

Best time: Nov–Mar Ideal trip: 4–6 days Perfect for: Luxury, Shopping, Couples

Quick Overview

Dubai is a world-famous luxury destination known for its futuristic skyline, world-class shopping, deserts, beaches, and incredible nightlife. Perfect for honeymoons, premium vacations & adventure.

Top Places to Visit

  • Burj Khalifa – World’s tallest building
  • Dubai Marina – yachts, cafes & skyline views
  • Desert Safari – dunes, camel rides, BBQ night
  • Palm Jumeirah – luxurious resorts
  • Dubai Mall – shopping, aquarium & dancing fountain
  • JBR Beach – beach clubs & watersports
  • Old Dubai – souks, creek & culture
