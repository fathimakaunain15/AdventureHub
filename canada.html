Why Canada?

Canada has some of the world’s most stunning landscapes — turquoise glacier lakes, snow-capped peaks, endless forests, wildlife, and crystal-clear blue water adventures. Banff and Lake Louise are the heart of the Canadian Rockies.

Top Highlights

  • Lake Louise — Bluest lake in the world
  • Moraine Lake — 10 Peaks Valley views
  • Banff Gondola Sky View
  • Wildlife spotting (Elk, Bears)
  • Frozen lakes in winter

Photo Gallery

3-Day Best Itinerary

Day 1 — Banff Town + Gondola

Explore Banff town, ride the Banff Gondola, enjoy mountain cafés and wildlife viewing.

Day 2 — Lake Louise

Visit Lake Louise, enjoy canoeing (summer) or ice skating (winter) & take picture-perfect views.

Day 3 — Moraine Lake

See the iconic blue waters of Moraine Lake & go on short hikes around the Ten Peaks Valley.

Top Stays

Fairmont Lake Louise

Luxury stay with lake views.

Banff Springs Hotel

Castle-style luxury resort.
